HOW I APPROACH A WORKFLOW
-------------------------
1. Understand the people, decisions, inputs and failure costs.
2. Remove unnecessary steps before automating anything.
3. Separate deterministic rules from probabilistic interpretation.
4. Keep evidence, review and accountability visible.
5. Evaluate the workflow, not only the model output.
6. Hand off explicit requirements, logic and quality criteria.
