    03 · Product method

    Start with the workflow, then choose the tools.

    Use AI for variable interpretation, rules for repeatability, and human review for context, risk and accountability.

    1. 01

      Frame

      +

    2. 02

    3. 03

    4. 04

      Prototype

      +

    5. 05

      Evaluate

      +

      Test quality, effort, failure modes and review thresholds.

    6. 06

      Operationalise

      +

      Set ownership, monitoring and feedback loops.

    Solution ladder

    Choose the least complex intervention that meets the need.

    These are options, not a maturity score. Move up only when the previous level cannot solve the workflow reliably.

    1. 01Simplify

      Remove steps, clarify the decision and improve the interface.

    2. 02Structure

      Standardise inputs, ownership, states and source data.

    3. 03Automate

      Use rules for repeatable validation, calculation and routing.

    4. 04Assist

      Use AI for variable language, retrieval, synthesis or explanation, with evidence and review.

    5. 05Orchestrate

      Connect tools, agents and handoffs around shared state and approval gates.

    6. 06Productise

      Add dedicated UX, permissions, evaluation, monitoring and support.

    Stop at any level. Added complexity is justified only when it improves a useful outcome.

    Responsible by design

    Set the boundaries before automating.

    • TraceabilityLink claims to sources and transformations.
    • UncertaintyRoute low-confidence cases to review.
    • AccountabilityName the owner, checkpoint and override.
    • SimulationUse synthetic audiences to test hypotheses — not replace research.

    Explain it simply

    I make complicated work easier.

    Imagine a desk covered in papers, messages and little jobs. I help turn that mess into one clear path.

    1. First, I listen.

      I learn what people are trying to do and what keeps getting in their way.

    2. Then, I tidy the work.

      I remove extra steps and give every piece of information a clear place.

    3. Only then, I add AI.

      AI helps read, write or spot patterns. Rules check the facts. A person makes important decisions.

    The goal

    Not more AI. Less confusion, less repetitive work and a result people can understand and check.

    This site shows examples of that thinking, a small tool you can try and the experience I bring.
