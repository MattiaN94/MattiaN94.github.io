    03 · Product method

    Understand the work before choosing the model.

    I use AI where interpretation creates leverage, deterministic logic where consistency matters and human review where context or accountability cannot be delegated.

    1. 01

      Frame

      Identify the user, the decision and the actual friction — not just the requested feature.

    2. 02

      Map

      Make inputs, handoffs, exceptions, policies and tacit judgement visible.

    3. 03

      Architect

      Assign each step to rules, data, AI or people based on risk and variability.

    4. 04

      Prototype

      Build the smallest end-to-end path that can be tested with real work.

    5. 05

      Evaluate

      Define evidence, failure modes, review thresholds and acceptance criteria.

    6. 06

      Operationalise

      Design ownership, feedback and the handoff from prototype to production.

    Responsible by design

    Automation needs boundaries, not just capabilities.

    • TraceabilityOutputs remain connected to their sources and transformations.
    • Explicit uncertaintyLow confidence becomes a review state, not a hidden guess.
    • Human accountabilityCritical decisions retain a named checkpoint and an override path.
    • Honest simulationSynthetic audiences explore hypotheses; they never replace real research.
